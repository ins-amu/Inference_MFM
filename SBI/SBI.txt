Here are examples of SBI over (deterministic and stochastic) mean-field model of spiking QIF neurons (https://arxiv.org/abs/1506.06581).

For installtation see: https://github.com/sbi-dev/sbi

The version used:  

Numpy version: 1.24.4
Numba version: 0.57.1
Matplotlib` version: 3.7.1
Pandas version: 2.0.2
SciPy version: 1.10.1
SBI version: 0.21.0

The function to calculate the data features is located in Helper.
